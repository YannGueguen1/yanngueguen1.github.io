About me

My name is Yann Gueguen, I have lived in Chicago, USA, since 2014, after moving from France to pursue my studies at Northwestern University.

After graduation, I started working as a structural engineer in Chicago, focusing on the design of steel connections on buildings all over the country, including several high rises and SpaceX's modification of launch pad LC-39A.

A friend introduced me to Web Development and I fell in love with the endless possibilities that this field contains. I am currently studying Full Stack Development and the MERN stack at the MIT xPRO, with a graduation in April 2022.
